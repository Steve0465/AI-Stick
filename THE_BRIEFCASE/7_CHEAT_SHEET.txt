QUICK FIXES:
────────────

Windows won't boot:
   1. Plug AI_STICK into laptop
   2. Boot from USB (press F9)
   3. Pick SystemRescue
   4. Terminal: cd /mnt/usb/windows_rescue
   5. Run: sudo ./ai_rescue.sh /dev/sda

Laptop won't stay on:
   1. Remove battery
   2. Unplug AC
   3. Hold power 60 seconds
   4. Plug AC only
   5. Try power on
